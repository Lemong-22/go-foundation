# high level requirements

create a comprehensive and cohesive learning engine/platform.
first would cater for coding, but later would cater for general education including STEM.
principles to follow:

- progressive disclosure of knowledge at several layers.
- bite-sized explanations, using html-explainer / sprite-animation / threejs / or ai-gen-videos.
- sandboxed assignments generated by AI and evaluated by AI, preferably linted/compiled when [submit] is clicked.
- quiz rotation for fast memorization
- easy to access notes possibly using mindmap
- system-design course (down the road)
- practical projects and step-by-step evolution.
- learning-data gathering, logging, and analytics for accurate / tuned guided learning.

# progressive disclosure principle

- paginated step-by-step evolution of concept.
- it gets complex as step progresses in time; 1/10 shows the concept at its embryonic stage.
- 2/10 is one step up of complexity, and directly tied to 1/10 in causality.
- 5/10 is more complex than 1/10.
